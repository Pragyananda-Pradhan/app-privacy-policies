Privacy Policy

This policy explains how the Calculator app handles information, including data retention, local data deletion, and account deletion.

  • App name: Calculator
  • Developer: Pragyananda-Pradhan
  • Effective date: May 21, 2026
  • Last updated: May 22, 2026
  • Privacy contact: GitHub issues for this policy repository

Summary

Calculator is designed to work locally on your device. The app does not create user accounts, does not require a login, does not sell personal data, and does not transmit your calculations to the developer or to external servers.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal or sensitive user data from the Calculator app. In this policy, "collect" means transmitting data from the app off your device to us or to a third party.

Your calculation inputs, calculation results, local history, and app preferences, if available in your version of the app, remain on your device and are not sent to us.

How the App Uses Information

The app uses the numbers and operators you enter only to perform calculator functions on your device. If the app stores calculation history or display preferences, that storage is local to your device and is used only to provide those app features.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal or sensitive user data with third parties because the app does not collect or transmit that data.

If you choose to copy, export, or share a calculation result using Android system features or another app, that action is controlled by you and the selected destination app. We do not receive the shared content.

App Permissions

The Calculator app does not need access to sensitive device permissions such as location, contacts, camera, microphone, phone, SMS, photos, calendar, or storage to perform basic calculations.

If a future version requests a new permission, this policy will be updated to explain what permission is requested, why it is needed, and how related data is handled.

Third-Party Services

This policy applies to a Calculator app that does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, cloud sync, or external APIs that collect user data.

The app is distributed through Google Play. Google may process information related to app downloads, purchases, reviews, device compatibility, diagnostics, and security under Google's own policies. That processing is separate from this app's privacy practices.

Data Security

Because the app does not transmit your calculations or personal data to us, we do not store your app data on developer-controlled servers. Local data is protected by your device, Android's app sandbox, and your own device security settings.

Data Retention, Account Deletion, and Local Data Deletion

We do not retain user data on our servers. Any calculation history or preferences stored by the app remain on your device until you delete them in the app, clear the app's data in Android settings, or uninstall the app.

The app does not create user accounts and does not store account data on developer-controlled servers. Because no account is created, there is no server-side account for us to delete.

To remove local app data from your device, open Android Settings, go to Apps, select Calculator, choose Storage, and tap Clear data. You can also uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If the app is later directed to children or families, the developer will review and update this policy and the app's Google Play declarations as required.

Your Choices

You can remove locally stored app data by clearing the app's data in Android settings or uninstalling the app. You can also contact us with privacy questions using the contact address listed above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes, when our data practices change, or when legal or Google Play requirements change. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app's privacy practices, contact us through this policy repository's GitHub issues page. If the Google Play listing provides a developer email address, you may also contact the developer through that email.
