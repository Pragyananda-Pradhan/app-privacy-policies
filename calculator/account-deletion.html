Calculator

Account Deletion

Calculator does not create user accounts or store account data on developer-controlled servers.

No Account Required

The Calculator app does not require sign-in, does not create user accounts, and does not store account data on our servers. Because no account is created, there is no server-side account for us to delete.

Delete Local App Data

If you want to remove data stored locally on your device, such as calculation history or app preferences if available, you can clear the app's data in Android settings or uninstall the app.

  1. Open Android Settings.
  2. Go to Apps.
  3. Select Calculator.
  4. Choose Storage.
  5. Tap Clear data, or uninstall the app.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us through this policy repository's GitHub issues page. If the Google Play listing provides a developer email address, you may also contact the developer through that email.
