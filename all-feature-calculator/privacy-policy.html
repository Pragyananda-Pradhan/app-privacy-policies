Summary

All Feature Calculator is designed to work locally on the user's Android device. The app does not create accounts, does not require login, does not sell personal data, and does not transmit app data to the developer or external servers.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal or sensitive user data from All Feature Calculator. In this policy, "collect" means transmitting data from the app off the user's device to us or to a third party.

User inputs, calculation results, finance scenarios, unit conversions, and local calculation history are processed on the device and are not sent to us.

How the App Uses Information

The app uses information entered by the user only to perform the calculation, conversion, graph, solver, or reference action requested by the user on the device.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal or sensitive user data with third parties because the app does not collect or transmit that data.

If the user manually copies a result or shares content through Android system features or another app, that action is controlled by the user and the selected destination app. All Feature Calculator does not receive or store the shared content.

Internet Access

The app does not request internet permission. All calculations are performed locally on the user's device.

App Permissions

All Feature Calculator does not request sensitive permissions such as location, contacts, camera, microphone, phone, SMS, photos, calendar, or external storage access.

Third-Party Services

This version does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, cloud sync, payment SDKs, login SDKs, or external APIs that collect user data.

The app is distributed through Google Play. Google may process information related to app downloads, purchases, reviews, device compatibility, diagnostics, and security under Google's own policies. That processing is separate from this app's privacy practices.

Data Security

Because the app does not transmit app data or personal data to the developer, the developer does not store app data on developer-controlled servers. Local app data is protected by Android's app sandbox and the user's own device security settings.

Data Retention, Account Deletion, and Local Data Deletion

The app does not create user accounts and does not store account data on developer-controlled servers. Because no account is created, there is no server-side account for the developer to delete.

Any local app history or preferences remain on the user's device until the user clears them, clears app data in Android settings, or uninstalls the app.

To remove local app data, open Android Settings, go to Apps, select All Feature Calculator, choose Storage, and tap Clear data. Users can also uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data.

Finance Tools Disclosure

The finance tools are educational calculators only. They do not offer loans, banking, trading, cryptocurrency services, insurance, payments, credit monitoring, financial products, or personalized financial advice. Users should verify important financial decisions with a qualified professional or institution.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If the app is later directed to children or families, this policy and the app's Google Play declarations should be reviewed and updated.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app changes, when data practices change, or when legal or Google Play requirements change. Updates should be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
